Healthcare Facility Restoration
In Wheeling, IL

Hospitals and other healthcare facilities require an experienced and well trained company to respond rapidly and efficiently. Whether the hospital was affected by fire, flood, tornado, hurricane, or earthquake, it is crucial to the staff, patients, and cleanliness of the building to respond rapidly and comprehensively. The Alliance Disaster Kleenup healthcare division specializes in the restoration of hospitals and medical facilities affected by fires, floods, mold, etc.

From restoring and/or rebuilding the structure to patient records and x-ray recovery, Alliance Disaster Kleenup is the single source solution.

Healthcare Focused Restoration Services

We provide a wide array of restoration services, project management, and infections control that are customized for the complex rigors of the healthcare industry. Alliance Disaster Kleenup technicians are trained to follow all privacy standards and JCAHO requirements for project management and documentation.
